Scoring Methodology
Each ticker is scored 1–5 on six factors. Hype Risk is inverted ↓ — heavy crowding lowers the composite.
Composite (0–100) = 20 × [ 0.25·EPS + 0.20·Val + 0.20·Narr + 0.15·(6 − Hype) + 0.15·Cat + 0.05·Liq ]
